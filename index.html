About Me

I'm a passionate and curious engineering student at ENSAM Rabat, driven by a strong interest in electronics, digital technologies, and smart systems. While I’m still exploring the many paths of electrical and embedded engineering, I enjoy learning by building—whether it's an interactive dashboard, a connected prototype, or a visual experience on the web.
